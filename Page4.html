Unity Flames

What is Unity Flames?

Unity Flames is a newly created studying technique hoping to be among the great and famous studying techniques out there in the world, the technique was created by students from Africa. The students had were enthusiasts on the methods for effective and efficient learning and studying and the creation of this technique acts as a reflection of what the students have mastered and seen from different studying techniques. The technique is derived from the principles of higher ordered learning some of which include relation, finding the why and application of what has been learnt, it also includes other traits of active recalling. This technique is very useful for it enables you to cover multiple topics or chapters, it tests your understanding through active recalling and it’s also a method of creating notes that are easy to review.


How to Use Unity Flames

1. On a paper you’re going to list the topics you covered and you’re going to draw lines of arrows where one topic or chapter is going to be connected to another and this will apply to all the topics listed. Note: The best way of doing this is to connect topics that have a certain relation even if it’s small, be it one concept from another topic is better than the other or the concepts they have are similar in a way.


2. On the next page draw a line having two columns you’ll put pair of topics.


3. On the first column write what you have learnt form the topic in your own words without looking at the learning material. Ensure it is kept as simple as possible.


4. Then on the second column write what you have learnt about the second topic paired with the first one written in your own words and without looking at the learning material and make sure it is kept as simple as possible. Note: The notes on the second column should be written in way that will relate to the notes on the first column.


5. Do this for all the topics or chapters listed from step one.This technique has been used for some time by the creators and used by learners of this new studying method. It has improved their studies dramatically by making note taking as easy to do, made notes save time on reveal and it challenged their understanding through doing this without looking at the learning material.
